The smart home that is eco-concious.

CERES, an environment education centre, tasked us with creating a smart home utilising IOT and green technology.

What we came up with was a system that is able to adapt and control the home for optimal energy efficiency based on environmental and behavioural factors.

For example, it would open the windows during the morning to let cold air in, then close them for the heat of midday to save energy on active cooling.
