The Magispace

Scroll for Design Story.


Exploring the reality of imagination.

What do you call home, and What is your connection to it?
We were taksed with creating a web project that reflected and expressed this thought.

The place I call home is rather different though. It's not physical.
The Magispace is a work that expresses my home in the imagination, featuring places that I visit, my interests and the realisation of Imagination as a space, place and home.
