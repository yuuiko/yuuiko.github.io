The Touch Bar
Vastly Improved.
AquaTouch reinvents the Touch Bar with a new UI, comprehensive, more understandable controls, and far more tools than you’ve ever seen before.

QuickStrip
What you need,
where you need it.

Your common actions in one place.
Control your currently playing media, check your schedule, and control your machine, all in here.

Gestures
Volume? Brightness?
No more blind guessing.

Stop hunting for that volume button you can’t feel.
AquaTouch Controls all that using gestures you can start anywhere on the touch bar, in one motion.

Notification Badges
No more missed messages.

You can get a notification bubble that stays put when you get a message, text or email!

App Support
Dev didn’t add Touch Bar?
I did it myself.

Control your Illustrator Tool. Check what Apple Music is playing. Get Spotify lyrics. Markup Preview Pictures. Switch Discord servers.

I threw many app UIs out the window, and added controls for over 55+ applications.
Just do it better already.

Website Controls
Carefully-crafted controls,
for websites too.

The amount of tools you get doesn’t stop there. AquaTouch adds support for your favourite websites too!

Activate Picture-in-Picture. Mark Gmail as read. Create a Twitch clip. Preview Google Drive Files. Gain a Kahoot advantage. Control SoundCloud Playback. Format Google Docs Paragraph

Better UI Design
Allows you to develop
Muscle Memory.

Intuitive
Controls have a more informative design than the “grey icons only” default look.

Ergonomic
Frequently used actions are always on the left, with comfortable sizing and easy reach.

Consitent
Controls are designed so that actions are always in the same place, AquaTouch stays put and doesn’t constantly switch around.

Elegant
Crisp icons, colour coding and elegant layouts just make sense, and look amazing too.
